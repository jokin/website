Overview

Sixth community meeting of the Nobodies Collective Asociación (NCA). Updates on asset procurement, the official event name announcement (“Elsewhere”), Humans app and VIM (volunteer management) progress, budgeting, recruitment status, ticketing, communications, and power team readiness.

Key Updates

Legal and Asset Procurement Progress (Pepe)

  • Productive progress on asset procurement paperwork.
  • Confident that a signed contract will be secured by early the following week, granting operational control of key assets.

Event Name Announcement (Pablo)

  • Following a community vote with statistical analysis, the official working title for the event is “Elsewhere”.
  • The name is effective immediately for all operational matters.
  • Final legal clearance expected within a few days.
  • All communications, assets, and channels moving to the Elsewhere identity.

Humans App and VIM Update (Peter)

  • Humans app live and functioning as the hub for managing teams, resources, and access. Roles recently added.
  • The system previously known as “Fist” has been renamed VIM (Volunteer Information Manager).
  • VIM getting a visual refresh and potential integration with Humans to avoid duplicate data entry. Integration feasibility being evaluated.
  • Not all collaborators need VIM/shifts — some roles (e.g. consent team) don’t have shifts.

Budgeting and Financial Forecast (Peter)

  • Bank account opening in progress — personnel visiting the bank daily to expedite.
  • Budget being divided into weekly cash flow analysis.
  • First payment due before May: asset deposit.
  • All other known payments are May or later.

Recruitment Status (Frank)

  • Coordinators secured for all departments but one.
  • Urgent open roles: ticketing lead (most immediate), strike lead, barrier support strike lead, asset coordinator.
  • A community member volunteered to help with ticketing.

Ticketing Update (Daniel)

  • Ticket sales aiming to open Saturday (March 14), possibly sooner.
  • Core contributors (people listed on the Humans app) will receive a discount code.
  • ~160 people currently signed up on the Humans app.
  • Humans app sign-up is not required to buy tickets; discount is recognition for contributors.
  • At similar events, core contributors typically receive early access; for this event it’s a discount since the ticketing platform doesn’t support restricted early sales.

Humans App Functionality (Daniel)

  • Tracks volunteers, collaborators, and members.
  • Manages GDPR compliance and IP agreements (NCA gets a licence to use contributed IP; contributors retain ownership).
  • Automates administrative tasks: creating mailing lists, assigning Drive permissions by team.
  • “Each one teach one” — community asked to get one more person to sign up in the next 48 hours.

Communications Team Update (read by Daniel)

  • Newsletter to go out once ticket dates are finalised.
  • Strong traction on WhatsApp (approaching 1,000 members) and Telegram.
  • Instagram to open once official NCA emails are set up.
  • Working on a new wiki-based survival guide.

Power Team Update (Cupcake)

  • All pre-event power roles covered: red grid, yellow grid, volunteer coordinator.
  • Team includes members with experience at similar events.
  • Looking for additional power architects.
  • Will begin reaching out to departments about power needs soon.

Financial Sustainability and Ticket Sales Projections (Peter)

  • Break-even point is approximately 70% of comparable event sales.
  • If ticket sales are drastically low by beginning of May, that would present a problem.
  • Spending will be adjusted based on actual ticket sales as they come in.
  • Organisation operating lean through automations and a larger contributor team.

Associate Membership and VAT Status (Daniel)

  • Currently only eight members (the board).
  • Associate approval paused until a statutes amendment allowing asynchronous board voting is passed (expected the following Monday).
  • Once passed, pending associate applications will be processed.
  • VAT: organisation will not bundle memberships with tickets (following tax advice). Currently operating at 10% VAT. Full nonprofit status (zero VAT on tickets) expected in a couple of years.

Logo and Branding

  • Ticket sales can proceed without a finalised logo.

Ticket Sales Channels

  • Tickets will be sold through all available channels, not limited to Telegram/WhatsApp.
  • All ticket sales and communications will use NCA’s own channels and domain.

Update zur Humans-App und VIM (Peter)

  • Die Humans-App ist aktiv und dient als zentrale Anlaufstelle für die Verwaltung von Teams, Ressourcen und Zugängen. Kürzlich wurden Rollen hinzugefügt.
  • Das zuvor als „Fist“ bekannte System wurde in VIM (Volunteer Information Manager) umbenannt.

  • Nicht alle Mitarbeiter benötigen VIM/Schichten — einige Rollen (z. B. Consent-Team) haben keine Schichten.
